For the Media

This area of our website is intended for the media. You can download our press kit, press releases, our most recent calendar and newsletter as well as images for our current film series.

As an active member of the Chicago film community, Doc Films is happy to work with media from outside the borders of Hyde Park. In the past, we have been featured in the Chicago Tribune, Chicago Magazine's Best of Chicago issue, Cahiers du Cinéma, as well as many other publications. If there's any information you would like to know about Doc Films that you can't find here, please let us know.

If you are interested in more information about specific Doc screenings or special events, please contact Becca Hall, Publicity Chair, at rfhall@uchicago.edu.
