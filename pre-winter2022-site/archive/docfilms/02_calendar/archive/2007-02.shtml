The Spring 2007 calendar and our Spring 2007 newsletter are now available in PDF form. We generally deliver the print calendars around Hyde Park, and to as many film and art hotspots around the rest of Chicago as we can. If you'd like us to deliver to a location we may not have thought about, especially in the Hyde Park/Kenwood/Woodlawn neighborhoods, please let us know.

This quarter, Doc would like to extend special thanks to Mike Mashon of the Library of Congress, Mari Hiruta and Keiji Shono of the Japan Foundation, Brian Meacham of the Academy Film Archive, and the Wisconsin Film Archive.

All foreign language films are screened with English subtitles. Series curated by Hannah Airriess, Anna Gunderson, Evan Chung, Aaron Greenberg, Kian Bergstrom, Joe Vidal, Lixian Hantover, and William Vail. Additional blurbs by Andrew Alger, Yana Morgulis, Tim Froh, Frank Bednarz, Fred Pfeiffer, Natan Dotan, and Kyle Westphal.
