Calendar: Week 11

March 16

Sunday, March 16 - 1:00
Enchanted
Kevin Lima, 2007 - 107 min.
It's packed with parodies of Disney fairytale classics, but Enchanted tells a tale as sweet as any of the ones it spoofs. When the animated Princess Giselle falls through a magic portal, she ends up in the alien world of live-action Manhattan (in the form of Junebug's Amy Adams). Navigating New York traffic, a dragon, and the cynicism of the divorce lawyer she befriends (Patrick Dempsy) with charming idealism, a CGI chipmunk, and many songs (from the folks who wrote Wicked), Giselle learns that true love can be as powerful in the real world as in any fairytale kingdom. 35mm.
Sunday, March 16 - 4:00
Beowulf
Robert Zemeckis, 2007 - 113 min.
Hwaet! No one expected Zemeckis and veteran cyberpunk screenwriter Neil Gaiman to adapt the Anglo-Saxon epic to film any more faithfully than featuring its most memorable battles as action sequences dripping with special effects. But in this, it does a good job. Grendel looks the part of a cadaverous draugr, the dragon appears lifted from a D&D sourcebook, and Angelina Jolie brings suppleness to a creature once defined solely by a vague sense of gruesomeness. And who won't quiver to see the most disarming scene in hallowed literature done justice? 35mm.
