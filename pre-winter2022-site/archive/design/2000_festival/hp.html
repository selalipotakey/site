Welcome to Hyde Park,
home to Doc Films and the University of Chicago. We hope that you will take this opportunity to explore one of Chicago's Great neighborhoods.

Hyde Park is a popular stop on many visitors' lists of Chicago's noteworthy neighborhoods, with cultural attractions such as the world-famous Museum of Science and Industry, the DuSable Museum, the David and Alfred Smart Museum of Art, and the leading-edge exhibits of the Renaissance Society.

If you're a fan of exotic dining, you'll be happy to know that ethnic cuisine is a staple in Hyde Park with Italian, Chinese, French, Thai, and Cajun food options. Dining choices in the neighborhood suit every taste, ranging from Chicago deep-dish pizza, through Lebanese, Mexican, and Korean delicacies, to standard Greek coffee shop fare. Those who love French pastry, sushi, barbequed ribs or bagels can find their favorite places here, too.

We hope you will take the time to explore Hyde Park during your visit to the Chicago International Film Festival @ Doc Films.
